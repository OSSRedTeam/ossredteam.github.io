Introduction

Welcome to Penetration Testing Essentials with OSS! Before we get started, I want to give a big thanks to the OSS team for putting this course together. In this course, we aim to teach you about the fundamentals of penetration testing. We will showcase different industry tools throughout the course such as Nmap, Wfuzz, ImPacket, crackmapexec, and much more! We have labs to assist you in applying what you learn along the way. At the end of the course you should be able to successfully assess and perform a penetration test against a target machine. We look forward to seeing you successfully complete this course and become a true penetration tester!

You can navigate this course using the left side bar. We recommend you follow the topics in order to fully understand the concepts. If you have any questions don’t hesitate to email us at contact@osscsuf.org
